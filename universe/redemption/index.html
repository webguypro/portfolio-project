Coram Deo "in the presence of God"

The Bible teaches that in our redemption our record does not change, but our guilt does. It is impossible for the Lord to forget anything, but when we are justified, He no longer holds our sin against us. If we are in Christ by faith, He no longer sees us as guilty. This does not mean we may sin with impunity, but it does mean that the truly justified will also seek to practice holiness in gratitude for their new status.
